Education

Since graduating from the European Institute of Cosmetology in Portland Oregon, my passion for hair has expanded within ongoing education classes through the years. I have gained inspiration through training with Bumble and Bumble, Goldwell, Wella, So.Cap USA hair extensions, Keratin Complex, Oribe, Davines, Joico, Unite, and Kevin Murphy to name a few. I find that the more knowledge I collect through education, the more I can offer my clients to be on-point, precise, and up to date with current trends. I look forward to sharing my experience and expertise with you!
